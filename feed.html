About Me


I moved seven times before the age of 19, calling home to New England, the South, the Midwest, Europe, and Asia. I work as a field operative on progressive campaigns because I'm obsessed with getting to know new people and places on an intimate, authentic level. As someone without a true "hometown", I'm inspired by those making positive change in their communities -- and love working as hard as I can to support and empower them.
